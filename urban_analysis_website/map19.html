Voluntary Work for an Organisation or Group by Age by Sex

Projected G19 Map

This interactive map showcases the data from G19; the TOTAL Voluntary work for an organisation by age and, by sex. From the 3D model, it is clear that both sides of the latte line project similar results. With the exception of areas such as Taronga Zoo and Sydney Airport (Black zones) and institutions such as Universities (UNSW, USYD) the number of volunteers across the given area is approximately the same.
